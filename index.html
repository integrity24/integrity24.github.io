FAQ

What is a crypto airdrop? +
"Airdrop" is another term for "free cryptocurrencies." To get these airdrops you usually have to do small tasks like joining a telegram chatgroup or following on Twitter. These free tokens are distributed by new companies. In most cases it is a so-called ERC20 tokens, a cryptocurrency based on the Ethereum platform. Therefore it is necessary that you have at least one Ethereum wallet address. For this we recommend www.myetherwallet.com (DO NOT use a wallet address of an exchange).
Why do companies give away free cryptocurrencies? -
Marketing! They form a large community because many people want these free tokens. The purpose of this is to draw people's attention to your project, so that they will eventually even buy tokens, since the project appeals to them.
And these free tokens have value? -
Yes! You might think just because you get these tokens for free they are not worth it. However, these tokens are usually distributed before the sale in order to carry out marketing, as already mentioned. As a rule, a maximum of 1% of all tokens are made available for an airdrop. The rest is still for sale. Thus also the free tokens have a value.
Where can I sell my airdropped crypto? -
On various exchanges. Since the cryptocurrencies mainly come from new companies, they are not yet listed on the big exchanges like Binance. However, there are dozens more small Exchanges. You can check the token's website or Twitter. New exchange listings are usually widely announced. Then you only have to create an account at the Exchange and you can usually exchange the tokens for Ethereum or Bitcoin. You can the click here for the various exchange site.
I didn't receive the free cryptos after completing the airdrop? -
That's normal! Almost all Airdrops are distributed after the successful token sale. This can take from a few weeks to several months. In a few cases no tokens are distributed, because the company fails to sell enough tokens and the project unfortunately fails. We publish a weekly report every Monday with tokens distributed this week.
